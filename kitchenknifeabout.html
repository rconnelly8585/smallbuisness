About Our Company:

After a couple months of prototyping, we are finally satisfied with our first set of kitchen knives.

These knives were inspired by classic French style knives.

Our knives have a traditionl style but implement modern materials and design features.

We use AEBL stainless steel which has a high toughness and corrosion resistance.

The handles are made of Richlite which is meant to replicate ebony but is easier to maintain.

The handles are constructed with corby bolts which provide mechanical strength.

Our knives are currently hand made using a technique called stock removal.

Since our knives are hand made their will be very slight differences between each knife that we make.
